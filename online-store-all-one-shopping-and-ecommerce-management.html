Online Store for all-in-one shopping and ecommerce management

Online Store is an easy to set up and use online retail feature available in the Camis 5 system.

Online Store is a fully integrated shopping experience for customers and robust ecommerce solution for your organization

Online Store by Camis makes it easy for your customers to shop from anywhere at any time and conveniently browse your retail store, then make a purchase, and track orders without leaving home. 

Data management and advanced reporting

Online Store by Camis is fully integrated into the Camis 5 reservations and management system and is accessible on-site or offsite. Built-in permission settings put you in control of who can access the management system and what they can have access to.  Data is in real-time, so you always stay on top of sales, inventory, and fulfillment information.

Expand your retail operation online

Getting started

Online Store is built into the Camis 5 system. We support the integration and configuration of the Camis 5 Online Store and provide training and ongoing support.

Build your store

The content management suite makes it easy to manage inventory, customize products offered and add descriptions, photos, and prices.

Optimized ecommerce solution

Designed with users in mind, managing your online store is easy and shopping is enjoyable.

Flexible payments & sales tracking

Payment is secure, flexible, and easy with a multitude of payment options, including gift cards. Online Store will manage and track sales and taxes for you.

Inventory and Fulfillment

Manage fulfillment yourself or have Camis provide warehousing, picking, and shipping of orders.

Branded for You

Every Online Store is custom branded to meet your branding guidelines; this includes branded sales receipts and order confirmations, that are automatically generated after a sale is completed.

Delivers a personalized customer experience

Online Store purchases are automatically linked to the customer’s My Account page where a history of past purchases and receipts can be accessed and where your customer can track the shipping of their most recent purchase.

Advanced reporting

Transactions are linked to the Advanced Reports functions of the Camis 5 system. Customizable reports and dashboards provide business intelligence.
