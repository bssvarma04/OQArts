Camis Awarded St. Lawrence Parks Commission Contract

Camis Inc. has been awarded a contract from the St. Lawrence Parks Commission for the provision of a Campground Reservation, Registration, and Point of Sale Retail System. The new contract commenced on December 14, 2021, and will continue through to March 31, 2024, with contract renewal options for 2 additional 1-year terms.

In fulfillment of the contract, Camis will provide a progressive online system and web application to support booking inquiries, attraction reservations, and campground reservations. The solution will also provide for an integrated retail point of sale and inventory system, equipped for e-commerce. A highlight of the solutions provided includes a feature-rich employee-facing interface – making daily transactions easier, backend management, and reporting that is highly configurable to the needs of the St. Lawrence Parks Commission operations team.

“As a former client, Camis is excited to welcome the St. Lawrence Parks Commission back. We look forward to providing solutions which will not only improve the experience for those booking campgrounds but also provide a robust back-end management solution,” said Doug Hall, president and founder of Camis.

The St. Lawrence Parks Commission is a segue to rich tourism, recreation, heritage, and cultural experiences in Eastern Ontario. Visitors and guests can expect a new user-friendly campground reservation and registration experience to look forward to for the 2022 season.
